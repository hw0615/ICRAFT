Green Business

In keeping with the green IT age, we are striving for environmentally friendly corporate management.

For the future of all of us, we will move towards green IT.

  • We reduce the amount of paper used in the company, eliminate unnecessary waste through recycling and separate collection.
  • We are striving to secure a healthy working environment by minimizing waste by practicing recycling of products.
  • We are committed to working with suppliers and vendors that meet the strictest environmental standards in the industry and strictly comply with domestic regulations and international regulations.
  • We provide employees with information on environmental considerations to strengthen their awareness of environmental management and reduce energy consumption for office space operations such as turning off lunchtime and saving PCs.
