C.I Concept & Guide Line

Using simple and modern logotype, ICRAFT CI is based on Humanism, it represents the direction of a company that pursues a better life.

The triangle type applied to 'I' Graphic Motif implies the new presentation for "Ubiquitous Network" and represents Icraft's corporate philosophy to find new direction and challenge progressively. Blue used as the main color represents Icraft's expertise and Red symbolizes the enthusiasm and pioneering spirit of Icraft's Ubiquitous Network.

Basic Logo Mark & Grid System
