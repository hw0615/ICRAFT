CSR

It is our social mission to benefit our customers with our technology and services and to contribute to society.

At present, all employees of ICRAFT are voluntarily deducting 0.5% of their salary for donate. With the motto of "Sharing the World", all employee are participating in donation culture throughout the company.

The amount collected each month is financially support Social Welfare Foundation for children with disabilities, the disabled, and small and poor families. The company constantly interacts with family members and children.

The voluntary fundraising culture of our employees has been ongoing since January 2001, and the company is actively supporting it.
